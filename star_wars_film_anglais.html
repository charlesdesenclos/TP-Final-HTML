The different Star Wars films

Prelogy (1999-2005) :

-Star Wars, episode I : The Phantom Menace by George Lucas, released in 1999 .
-Star Wars, episode II : Attack of the Clones by George Lucas, released in 2002 .
-Star Wars, episode III : Revenge of the Sith by George Lucas, released in 2005.

Original trilogy (1977-1983) :

-Star Wars, episode IV : A New Hope by George Lucas, released in 1977 .
-Star Wars, episode V : The Empire Strikes Back by Irvin Kershner, released in 1980 .
-Star Wars, episode VI : Return of the Jedi by Richard Marquand, released in 1983.

Third trilogy (2015-2019) :

-Star Wars, episode VII : The Force Awakens by J. J. Abrams, released in 2015 .
-Star Wars, episode VIII : The Last Jedi by Rian Johnson, released in 2017 .
-Star Wars, episode IX : The Rise of Skywalker by J. J. Abrams, released in 2019.

Derivative films (since 2008) :

-Star Wars: The Clone Wars by Dave Filoni, released in 2008.
-Rogue One: A Star Wars Story by Gareth Edwards, released in 2016.
-Solo : A Star Wars Story by Ron Howard, released in 2018.
